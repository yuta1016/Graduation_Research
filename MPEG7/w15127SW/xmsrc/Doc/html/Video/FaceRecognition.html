Summary This component generates a FaceRecognition descriptor for face recognition and query. There is no other function related to this component. The descriptor uses eigenface method to extract face features.
Strong Points Computational simplicity and accuracy
Limitations The face images should be normalized before feature extraction. The positions of two eyes should be at (24,16) and (24,31) in the scaled image(56 pixels in height and 46 pixels in width)
